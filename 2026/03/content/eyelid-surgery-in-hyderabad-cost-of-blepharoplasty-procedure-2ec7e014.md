What Is Eyelid Surgery (Blepharoplasty)?
Blepharoplasty is a cosmetic surgical procedure that improves the appearance of the upper and/or lower eyelids. It removes excess skin, tightens loose muscle, and eliminates puffiness or under-eye bags. The result is a more youthful, alert, and rejuvenated look.
