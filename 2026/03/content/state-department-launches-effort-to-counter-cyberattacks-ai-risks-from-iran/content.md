# State Department launches effort to counter cyberattacks, AI risks from Iran, others 

Source: [Ben's Bites](https://abcnews.com/Politics/state-department-launches-effort-counter-cyberattacks-ai-risks/story?id=131265350)

---

The U.S. State Department has launched the Bureau of Emerging Threats to counter possible cyberattacks by Iran and risks posed by AI.
